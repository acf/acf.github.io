Hello,

The challenges associated with “aging in place” are many. Decreased mobility can make it more difficult to take care of oneself and increase the risk of falls. Beyond that, living alone can lead to depression. Luckily, there are ways to grow old at home both safely and happily.

I’d like to write an article for your readers about “aging in community.” The article is totally free. In it, I can talk about a variety of topics, including:

Senior roommates and other affordable housing options,
Home modifications for seniors who want to age in place, and/or
“Village-to-village” models that provide support and resources to aging adults in their own communities.

If you’re open to receiving an article, please let me know.

Thank you for your consideration!
